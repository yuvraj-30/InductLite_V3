Modern Construction Safety Software

Today’s biggest worksite challenges, solved.

Stop the induction bottlenecks

Big crews shouldn’t mean big delays or big safety risks. But paper inductions? That’s a queue waiting to happen. Standardise and speed up the process with digital sign-on and mobile inductions that workers complete straight from their phone. No lines, no confusion, no “Where’s that form?” Everyone starts safe. Everyone starts faster.

See your whole site, instantly

No more detective work. No more chasing subcontractors to finish safety tasks. A live worker directory and digital records tell you exactly who’s on site, who’s cleared, what’s complete, and what needs your attention – all in real time. Less waiting, less wondering, more getting stuff done.

Syncing the tools you use everyday

Construction tech should lighten your load, not double it. Breadcrumb integrates directly with Procore so every safety and project record stays accurate, up to date and in sync. Update once, and the rest happens automatically. Hours saved, fewer panics, more high-fives all round.
